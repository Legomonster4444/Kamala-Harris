My Stance

Education

Kamala Harris is going to improve our education system by expanding technical education and career opportunities. She believes we should put less pressure on standardized testing, so students can perform at the best of their abilities. She acknowledges that teachers do not receive the appreciation they deserve, and has decided to improve teachers working conditions and give every teacher a $13,500 raise. Kamala Harris will push the College for All Act that makes all 4-year colleges and universities free to attend. She will invest in these colleges and universities so they still get the money the need to continue providing education. She will clear student loans as they are a burden that we carry.

Inflation

One of Kamala Harris' goals is to control inflation. A few ways she will do this is by reducing essential costs such as food and housing. She has decided to raise the minimum wage so everyone can have a better living standards. She is also going to build around 3 million houses in the next four years to accommodate our growing population.

Healthcare

Kamala Harris believes in affordable healthcare for all, and will accomplish this goal by creating a healthcare insurance system where the government provides money for your health. Private health insurance companies can still offer their services, except everybody will have access to it through the government. She will reduce prescription drug costs.
